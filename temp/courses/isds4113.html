Information Technology Project Management Course Description:  Topics on effectively managing information technology projects including: setting goals and objectives; work breakdown structures; project scheduling; managing project resources; evaluation and review; incentives and qualitative analysis; project accounting; extensive use of cases involving hands-on computer analyses with state-of-the-art project management software.   Assignments:
  • Work Breakdown Structure
